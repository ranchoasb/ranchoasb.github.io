About Rancho ASB

Welcome!

We are the Associated Student Body (ASB) of Rancho San Joaquin Middle School, a group of students dedicated to serving and improving our community. Some of our main responsibilities include:

  • Organize school-wide activities
  • Promote school spirit and positive school culture
  • Develop leadership and communication skills
  • Work to benefit the students of Rancho and make our school a better place!
